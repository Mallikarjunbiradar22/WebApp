Protecting the Digital World

Stay Safe in Cyberspace

Cybersecurity protects computers, networks, and data from cyber attacks. Strong security practices help individuals and organizations safeguard sensitive information and maintain trust in the digital world.

🔒 Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords with letters, numbers, and special characters for every account.

🛡 Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA to add an extra layer of protection beyond your password.

📧 Beware of Phishing

Never click suspicious links or share personal information through unknown emails.

💾 Backup Your Data

Keep regular backups to protect your important files from ransomware or hardware failure.
